project idea

Where you can "smell" the creativity in the air, if any

initial idea

Initially, we were particularly excited about these two concepts: (1) A project management website/application designed to help users efficiently manage their projects and track progress; and (2) An interactive school map website that displays real images of buildings and estimates travel time between two locations.

improved idea

After careful consideration, our group decided to move forward with both projects, but from a different perspective: A simple website to help HUST students organize their class schedules. We also hope to add a 2D map showing the locations of their classes, making it easier for students to visualize where they need to be.
